Letter From My Heart 💌

Dear Anya, If someone had told me back in November that one conversation would slowly become one of the most meaningful parts of my life, I probably wouldn't have believed them. You walked into my life so unexpectedly. At first, you weren't looking for a relationship, and I respected that. Yet somehow, destiny quietly brought us closer. Day by day, our conversations became something I looked forward to more than anything else. We weren't perfect. We had misunderstandings, moments of distance, and times when everything felt uncertain. Some of those mistakes were mine, and if there's one thing I've learned, it's how valuable you truly are to me. Today, when I look back, I don't remember only the difficult moments. I remember the laughter, the random conversations, the comfort of knowing you're there, and the happiness you unknowingly bring into my life every single day. Thank you for being you.

Neither of us knew that a random conversation would slowly become one of the most beautiful parts of our lives. I still remember how you used to say you didn't want a relationship. Funny, isn't it?
